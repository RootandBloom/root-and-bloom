About Rebecca

Rebecca McLaren is the founder of Root and Bloom. A passionate gardener based in Moorland, Somerset, Rebecca brings years of hands-on experience and a deep love for nurturing small gardens.

After leaving horticultural college with a triple distinction, Rebecca began a remarkable apprenticeship on the island of Sark, working alongside a former Kew Gardens professional. There, she mastered the art of no-dig, organic gardening and maintained the gardens that supplied fresh produce to the prestigious Stocks Hotel throughout the season.

Since then, she has continued to grow in the horticultural world—currently working for a well-established landscaping company in Wales, where she maintains the grounds of many important clients. With over three years of experience, Rebecca now wishes to offer her skills directly to those who truly need them—individuals seeking help with their own garden spaces.

Hard-working, conscientious, friendly, and able to travel, Rebecca finds joy in working one-on-one with clients, helping them reclaim and fall in love with their outdoor spaces.
